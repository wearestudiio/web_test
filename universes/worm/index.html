Universe 001

WORM — elastic bloom

Industrial romance. Club lights like bioluminescent moss. Basslines that bend like a spine learning to dance again.

Motion signature: Elastic bloom

Manifesto

Shed the shell. Leave the shine. Echoes belong to the pit. Neon loves the rust. Glow is earned. Bass is spine. Bodies spiral, never bow. Every chorus mutates. No copy survives without the signature.

Do

  • Layer metallic percussion with warm vocal stacks.
  • Use greens and violets like bruises turning into light.
  • Invite collaborators who honour grime and tenderness.
  • Sign every stem; archive with catalog numbers.

Don’t

  • No pristine perfection—leave the human breath.
  • No luxury clichés; opulence is underground.
  • Never release without the WORM seal.
  • Don’t flatten dynamics; keep the elastic snap.
