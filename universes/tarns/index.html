TARNS — razor clarity

Glacial neon. Metal choirs. Angles that slice and then hug. Clarity is a blade with a velvet handle.

Motion signature: Crisp wipe

Manifesto

See the lake freeze, then cut. Every line is intentional. Negative space is sacred. Silence can be steel. Choirs shimmer like glass under pressure. Only signed clarity enters the archive.

Do

  • Use electric blues and molten peach for contrast.
  • Let vocals stack like mirrors facing mirrors.
  • Invite collaborators who love minimal precision.
  • Stamp every deliverable with the TARNS seal.

Don’t

  • No clutter. No muddy reverb.
  • No overuse of gold; sheen must be earned.
  • No unsigned stems in the wild.
  • Don’t rush the wipe—transitions must be crisp.
