What is a digital signature?

A cryptographic autograph. Each signed package references the artist’s universe contract. Fans can verify in seconds.

Unsigned work is beautiful myth, but it isn’t canon. The seal is the difference between souvenir and scripture.

In the AI era, provenance is taste armor. Chain entries prevent knock-offs from confusing the archive.

Signatures are lightweight, transparent, and respect privacy. Fans see proof without giving up anything personal.
