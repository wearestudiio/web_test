Authenticity

If it isn’t signed, it isn’t canon.

Digital signatures prove provenance. studiio anchors every official output to a blockchain registry so fans and collaborators can instantly verify the canon.

What is a digital signature?

A cryptographic proof that a package was created within the universe rules and approved by the artist or their trusted steward.

Contract of taste → Signature → Canon

Why blockchain provenance?

Proof in an AI era

Anyone can generate. Few can prove origin. Canon is the difference.

Portable trust

Collectors and collaborators can verify signatures without asking a platform to vouch.

How we seal canon

Intent captured

Universe prompts and boundaries are confirmed.

Human curation

The studiio team reviews and refines outputs.

Signature anchored

Hash + metadata recorded on-chain; public lookup available.
